Welcome to Match Me

How to Play

Each Round (1-3) will display cards face down.

The timer will begin when you click on Begin Game

Click on 2 cards. If a match is made, the cards will remain visible. If a match is not made, the cards will flip back over. Keep making matches to beat the timer.

If you make all the matches before the timer runs out, you will be able to advance to the next round by clicking on Next Round

If you run out of time, you lose the round. If you lose the round, you may Retry or go to the next round

If you complete all the rounds and beat the timer, YOU ARE AWESOME!

If you do not complete all the rounds and beat the timer, YOU ARE AWESOME but KEEP TRYING and don't forget to HAVE FUN!
